Privacy Policy for ChawaPete

Effective Date: December 9, 2024
Last Updated: December 9, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to ChawaPete ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you use our dating application.

By using ChawaPete, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender
  • Profile Information: Photos, bio, interests, preferences, relationship goals
  • Matching Preferences: Age range, distance preferences, gender preferences
  • Communications: Messages you send and receive through our chat feature
  • Payment Information: Subscription and payment details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (swipes, likes, matches, time spent)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Location Data: Approximate location (city/region) to help you find matches nearby
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visits

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media: If you sign up using Facebook or Google, we may receive basic profile information from these platforms

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Create and manage your account, show your profile to potential matches
  • Facilitate Connections: Enable matching, messaging, and other dating features
  • Improve Experience: Personalize content and recommendations based on your preferences
  • Communication: Send notifications about matches, messages, and app updates
  • Safety & Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and other harmful activities
  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with our app to improve features
  • Marketing: Send promotional content about premium features (you can opt out)
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Other Users

  • Your profile information (name, photos, bio) is visible to other ChawaPete users
  • Your messages are visible only to users you match with

3.2 With Service Providers

We work with third-party companies that help us operate ChawaPete:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: Data storage and authentication
  • Payment Processors: Stripe, Google Play, Apple Pay for subscriptions
  • Analytics Services: To understand app usage and improve features
  • Cloud Storage: For storing photos and user content

3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or to:

  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Protect the safety of our users

3.4 Business Transfers

If ChawaPete is acquired or merged with another company, your information may be transferred to the new owners.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage Location: Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Firebase (Google Cloud)
  • Security Measures: We use encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits
  • Data Retention: We retain your information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time, and we will remove your personal data within 30 days

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your profile information in the app settings

5.2 Delete Account

You can permanently delete your account by going to Settings > Account > Delete Account

5.3 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email

5.4 Location Data

You can disable location services in your device settings

5.5 Data Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us

6. Children's Privacy

ChawaPete is only available to users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a child under 18 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using ChawaPete, you consent to this transfer.

8. Third-Party Links

ChawaPete may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Displaying a notice when you open the app

Your continued use of ChawaPete after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
